About US

We are a group of enthusiasts who were passionate about the idea of creating our own blockchain network. But besides this, we decided to entrust another important mission to our shoulders - to explain to the user in the most accessible language what a blockchain is. We will try to describe everything, down to the line-by-line commentary of the blockchain network program code. Thanks to this, every reader will have not just a general idea of the blockchain, but will also be able to reproduce it themselves!

Advantages of the project

  1. The community determines the vector of development

  2. Developers implement your ideas

  3. We will help you immerse yourself in blockchain
